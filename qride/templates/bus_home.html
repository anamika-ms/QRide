Welcome to Operator Dashboard

This dashboard is designed to help bus operators manage their services with ease. From registering buses to creating routes and issuing tickets, everything you need is available in one place. Your role ensures a smooth and digital travel experience for passengers.

You can view quick statistics, track daily ticket sales, and update route information whenever required. Keeping your data accurate helps in better fare collection and a seamless experience for travelers.
